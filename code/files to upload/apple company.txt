Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California, that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. It is considered one of the Big Four technology companies, alongside Amazon, Google, and Microsoft.Apple was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in April 1976.Macintosh,iPod,iPhone,iPad,Apple Watch,Apple TV,HomePod,Software,Electric vehicles are the products of Apple Company.
