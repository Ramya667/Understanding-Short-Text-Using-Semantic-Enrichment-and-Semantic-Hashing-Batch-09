The Bajaj Avenger is made for those who love riding. It is perfect for long rides where the rider is one with the road. With its upright posture with low slung seat and unique extended footrest position, Avenger gives maximum riding comfort. Be it the cold of the Himalayas, the heat of the Thar Desert or the dampness of the Ghats, the Bajaj Avenger is sure to make the rider Feel like God.
