Orange is a 2010 Telugu romantic comedy-drama film directed by Bhaskar in his third venture after Bommarillu and Parugu.The film features Ram Charan, Genelia, and Shazahn Padamsee in the lead roles, while Sanchita Shetty, Prabhu, and Prakash Raj play supporting roles. The film, whose music was composed by Harris Jayaraj, began the first schedule in February 2010, and was released on 26 November 2010. Orange created first day all time record in Tollywood, and the album of this movie became a classic. Though the movie did not do well at the box office, it has gained cult status from fans who appreciated its new-age love story.
